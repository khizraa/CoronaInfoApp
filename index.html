What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by the novel coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2). It first emerged in December 2019 in Wuhan, China, and has since spread globally, leading to a pandemic.

    Key Points:

  1. The virus spreads primarily through respiratory droplets.
  2. It can also spread by touching contaminated surfaces and then touching the face.

Symptoms of COVID-19

  • Common Symptoms
    • Fever
    • Dry cough
    • Fatige
  • Severe Symptoms
    • Difficulty breathing
    • Chest pain or pressure
    • Loss of speech or movement

Preventive Measures

  • Wash hands regularly with soap for at least 20 seconds.
  • Wear a face mask in crowded places.
  • Maintain at least 6 feet of social distance.
  • Avoid touching your face, especially eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces daily.
